Izinyathelo zokulungiselela:
Ikhasi, hlanza futhi usike imifino. Izingcezu kufanele zibe usayizi ophakathi isikhathi esincane sokubilisa.
Faka ilitha lamanzi ebhodweni. Sebenzisa iso lesitofu esikhulu.
Engeza isilimo esidliwayo esinamagatsha anamanzi kanye ne-parsnip.
Vula iso lesitofu. Sebenzisa ilangabi eliphezulu noma izinga lokushisa eliphezulu.
Ngemva kokuba amanzi eseqale ukubila, shiya ukuthi abile cishe imizuzu eyi-10(?).
Engeza izingcezu ze-carrot. Engeza amanzi kuze kube yilapho zonke izingcezu zicwilisiwe. Xuba kahle.
Shiya ukuthi ubilise cishe imizuzu eyi-10(?).
Engeza izingcezu zamazambane. Engeza amanzi kuze kube yilapho zonke izingcezu zicwilisiwe. Xuba kahle.
Shiya ukuthi ubilise cishe imizuzu engama-20(?).
Engeza uphizi oluhlaza ojiyile kanye nama-pods aluhlaza. Engeza amanzi kuze kube yilapho zonke izingcezu zicwilisiwe. Xuba kahle.
Ngemuva kokuthi amanzi eqale ukubila, shiya ukuthi ubilise cishe imizuzu emi-5(?).
Engeza i-bell pepper, utamatisi, i-dill, i-parsley, ne-lovage bits.
Faka usawoti nezinongo. Engeza amanzi kuze kube yilapho zonke izingcezu zicwilisiwe. Xuba kahle.
Ngemva kokuba amanzi eseqale ukubila, shiya ukuthi abile cishe imizuzu eyi-10(?).
Vala iso lesitofu. Shiya ukuhlala cishe imizuzu engama-20-30.
